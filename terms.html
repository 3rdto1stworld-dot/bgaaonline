Democratizing Creativity Through Open-Source Engineering Resources

Our Mission: We exist to close the gap between imagination and reality. These terms support that mission while protecting our community.

Welcome to BGAA Engineering ("we," "our," or "us"). We're not a typical engineering company—we're curators, facilitators, and enablers of creativity. Our platform systematically organizes open-source engineering resources to make physical creation accessible to everyone.

By using our services, you're joining a community dedicated to democratizing engineering creativity. These terms explain how we operate and what we expect from our community members.

1. Our Open-Source Philosophy

Curated Open-Source: We don't create most content ourselves. We find, verify, organize, and present open-source engineering resources to make them practically useful.

What we provide:

  • CAD Library: STEP files curated from open-source projects worldwide
  • Tutorials: Collected and organized learning resources from the maker community
  • Product Directory: Links to third-party shops with engineering tools/materials
  • Future Manufacturing: Experimental systems we're building (currently in testing)

Note: We attribute all open-source content to original creators when attribution information is available. If you're a creator and find your work here without proper attribution, please contact us immediately.

2.1 File Format & Usage

Our CAD library exclusively provides .STEP files—the universal format for 3D modeling interoperability. These files are:

  • Ready for integration into your CAD projects
  • Modifiable in any major 3D modeling platform
  • Intended as building blocks for your designs

2.2 User Uploads

We welcome community contributions! When you upload designs:

  • We review all submissions for safety and quality
  • Approved designs receive a creator tag with your name
  • We may modify designs for standardization or improvement
  • You retain your original rights but grant us curation rights

Creator Promise: We will not use your designs for commercial profit without involving you in the process and benefits.

3. Manufacturing System (In Development)

We're building something special—a manufacturing system that makes physical creation truly accessible. Important notes about our current phase:

Experimental Phase: Our CNC machine with router/laser end-effectors and other rapid prototyping systems are currently in debugging and testing. We're engineers building engineering tools—perfection is our destination, but innovation is our continuous journey.

3.1 Realistic Expectations

When our manufacturing service launches (coming soon), please understand:

  • We strive for excellence but acknowledge we're building complex systems
  • Iteration and improvement are built into our DNA
  • Early adopters help us refine and improve
  • We're transparent about capabilities and limitations

3.2 Future Service Terms

When fully operational, our manufacturing service will:

  • Require design validation before production
  • Provide material recommendations based on your design
  • Offer transparent pricing based on complexity and materials
  • Maintain quality standards appropriate for prototyping vs production

4. Shop & Third-Party Services

Current Reality: Our "shop" is a curated directory, not a direct store.

How It Works: When you select a product, we redirect you to a third-party shop's WhatsApp for ordering. We're currently publicity partners helping quality engineering suppliers reach makers.

4.1 Important Disclaimers

  • We don't handle transactions – All purchases are directly with suppliers
  • We don't manage inventory – Stock availability is controlled by suppliers
  • We don't process refunds – See our separate Refund Policy for details
  • We curate, they deliver – We select quality suppliers, they fulfill orders

Supplier Relationships: We're selective about which suppliers we promote, but ultimately you're establishing a direct relationship with them when you purchase.

5. Intellectual Property & Creativity

Community First: We believe ideas want to be free, but creators deserve credit. Our approach balances open sharing with proper attribution.

5.1 Curation vs Creation

We make important distinctions:

  • Curated Content: Open-source resources we organize – original licenses apply
  • Platform Content: Our organization system, tutorials structure, metadata – our IP
  • User Contributions: Designs you upload – you retain rights with curation grant
  • Manufacturing Process: Our methods and systems – our proprietary approach

5.2 Attribution Promise

We commit to:

  • Always attribute open-source creators when information is available
  • Never claim others' work as our own
  • Encourage derivative work with proper attribution chains
  • Support the open-source ecosystem that enables us

6.1 Acceptable Use

Use our resources to:

Don't use our resources to:

We prefer collaboration over conflict. If issues arise:

  • Work toward constructive solutions

Engineering Mindset: These terms, like our systems, will evolve. We're building in the open, learning as we go, and committed to making creation accessible. If something doesn't make sense or seems counter to our mission, tell us. We're engineers—we fix things.

Effective Date: These terms are effective as of our continuous deployment. We don't have version numbers—we have iterations.
